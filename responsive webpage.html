The College

S.E.S. Polytechnic, Solapur, established in 1984 by the Solapur Education Society (founded 1931), is a premier diploma-level institute approved by AICTE and affiliated with MSBTE Mumbai.
Admissions: Admissions are merit-based, handled through the Maharashtra DTE CAP counseling process. Fees: Fees for courses are generally around INR 18,000 to 19,000 per year.
Infrastructure: The 6-acre campus features laboratories, a library with over 40,000 volumes, a computer center, a smart classroom, a canteen, and hostel facilities.
Placements & Training: The training and placement cell facilitates internships and placements with companies such as Tata, Kirloskar, and L&T. Staff: The institute is recognized for its helpful and experienced faculty.

Institute Vision

To provide quality technical education relevant to modern needs and to build overall Character of Student community.


Institute Mission

M1:- To impart technical knowledge through modern teaching methodology and well-equipped laboratories.
M2:-To update knowledge of students by providing useful learning resources.
M3:-To organize various training programs for the students to fulfill the needs of industry and society.
M4:-To facilitate the development of student's attitude and behavior through co-curricular and extracurricular activities.
